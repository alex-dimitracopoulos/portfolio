# P1: $NRG Portal Redesign

## What
A redesigned community portal for BLOND:ISH's $NRG Solana token. 
This is a working visual prototype — not a real Web3 app. 
Wallet connection and token data are mocked/hardcoded.

## Purpose
Show token holders their current access level and what holding 
more $NRG unlocks. Communicate the artist-fan relationship 
BLOND:ISH is building — exclusive access to her time, events, 
and community. The current portal is bare. This should feel 
like a venue, not a dashboard.

## Key sections
- Hero: $NRG logo/wordmark, mocked token price + market cap, 
  tagline that captures the energy philosophy
- Access Tiers: 3–4 tiers based on holding amount. Each tier 
  unlocks specific perks: backstage passes, pre-show 5km runs, 
  track feedback sessions, educational content, community calls
- Active Perks: what the current wallet holder has access to 
  (mocked as a mid-tier holder)
- Community pulse: minimal activity feed or recent events list

## Aesthetic
Dark, immersive. Underground electronic music energy — not 
corporate Web3 / crypto dashboard. Abracadabra Records label 
vibes. Reference: the feeling of a green room or an invite-only 
afterparty, not a SaaS product.

## Stack
Next.js 16, React 19, Tailwind v4 (@import "tailwindcss" syntax), 
TypeScript. No external dependencies unless already installed.
